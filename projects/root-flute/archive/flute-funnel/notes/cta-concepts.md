# CTA Concepts — Phase Two Flute Funnel

## Primary CTAs Used
- "Request This Instrument →" (current drop panel, links to #commission)
- "Submit Application" (commission form submit button)
- "Join to be the first to know." (below gallery, links to #community)

## CTA Positioning Logic
- Current drop CTA links INTO commission form (#commission anchor) — single-page funnel
- Gallery drives urgency before the form
- Scarcity lines flank both CTAs (25 tusks remain / availability is limited)

## CTA Ideas for Dedicated Funnel Page
- Waitlist / collector capture: "Join the Waitlist for the Next Drop"
- Inquiry flow: "Begin Your Commission" (softer than "Apply")
- High-ticket framing: "Request a Private Consultation"
- Scarcity drop: "Notify Me When Available"
- Mobile-first: sticky bottom CTA bar on mobile
